Team Coordination Philosophy
Communication and collaboration between staff are essential to provide services to LifeMoves clients. Team meetings allow for relationship building, alignment of goals and progress, problem solving and fostering the well-being of staff and the team. LifeMoves meeting philosophy is rooted in the principles of open communication, active listening, and mutual respect. Teamwork serves as the driving force
